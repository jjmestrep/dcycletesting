Frequently asked questions

How is pricing determined?

Pricing is based on your company size, complexity of your value chain, and the features you need. We offer flexible plans that grow with your sustainability program. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your specific requirements.

What's included in all plans?

All plans include access to our core platform with ISO 14064-certified carbon footprint calculations, basic ESG data management, standard compliance reports (GRI, CDP), and email support. The platform is fully hosted with automatic updates and EU data residency.

Can I upgrade or change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time as your needs evolve. Our team will help you transition smoothly, ensuring all your historical data is preserved. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.
