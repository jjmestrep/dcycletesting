Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, changes take effect at the end of your billing period.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for both Starter and Professional plans. No credit card required. You'll have full access to all features in your chosen plan during the trial.

What's included in the onboarding?

All plans include self-service onboarding with video tutorials and documentation. Professional plans include 2 onboarding sessions with our team. Enterprise plans get fully customized onboarding programs.

How does user pricing work?

Each plan includes a set number of users. Starter includes up to 50 users, Professional up to 200 users. Need more? Enterprise plans offer unlimited users, or contact us to add extra users to any plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and bank transfers for annual plans. Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice with NET-30 terms.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We're ISO 27001 certified with data stored in EU-based servers. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Enterprise plans include additional security features like SSO and audit logs.
